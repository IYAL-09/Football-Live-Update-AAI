How to Use This Site

Using Football News Update AAI is simple. The homepage displays today's live matches by default, grouped by league with real-time updates. You can scroll to view matches by kickoff time, league name, or team.

At the top, you'll find a horizontal date bar to switch between previous, live, today, or upcoming matches. Each match row shows the kickoff time, both team names, logos, and a star icon for marking favorites.

Clicking on a match opens a detailed view where you can explore live statistics, goals, substitutions, and even league standings. The hamburger menu lets you quickly return to the dashboard or send feedback to help us improve the experience.

No registration or login is required. Just visit the site, get updates, and enjoy live football — anytime, anywhere.
