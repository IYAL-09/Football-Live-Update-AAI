Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and serve relevant advertisements.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device by your browser. They help us understand user behavior, save preferences, and ensure the website functions smoothly.

How We Use Cookies

  • To remember your preferences and settings
  • To measure and improve site performance
  • To analyze traffic and user interactions (anonymously)
  • To display relevant advertisements through third-party ad partners

Third-Party Services

Some cookies may be set by third-party tools and advertising partners that help us understand site performance and deliver personalized ads. These services may use cookies or device identifiers to collect anonymous data.

Your Consent and Control

If you are visiting from the EU, UK, or other regions with privacy laws, a cookie banner will appear, allowing you to accept or reject cookie usage. You can also manage cookies anytime in your browser settings.

Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies in your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect how parts of this site function.

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Site Settings > Cookies
  • Firefox: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data

To learn more, visit Google’s Cookie Policy.
